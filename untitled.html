Introduction

The "Big Data Driven Secure and Smart Future Networks" workshop at ICCCN 2024 is a call to action for experts, scholars, and professionals to tackle the security and intelligence challenges brought about by the rise of big data. The paradigm of future networks will shift from "best effort" to deterministic, aiming to support seamless and highly reliable communications in the space-terrestrial, industrial internet, self-driving, generative AI and metaverse domains. Big data offers AI vast learning resources and advanced analytical opportunities, but also imposes challenges related to data quality, model interpretability, and the complexity of data processing and analysis. Moreover, big data, with its vast scale and complexity, introduces an expanded attack surface for malicious actors, making traditional security protocols insufficient. This, coupled with the intricacies of data privacy laws and the potential for data breaches, positions security as a critical concern. Our workshop spotlights the urgency of pioneering advanced security measures and intelligence solutions that leverage the power of big data itself, as well as artificial intelligence and machine learning, to create smarter and securer future networks.
